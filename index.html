Core product design rules

Useful principles to keep in mind when designing experiences.

01

Start with the user problem

Good design begins with understanding the real need before exploring a solution.

02

Reduce friction

Make the next step obvious and remove unnecessary complexity wherever possible.

03

Design for consistency

Reusable patterns help people understand the experience faster and trust it more.

04

Validate early

Prototype quickly and test ideas before spending too much time on polished work.

05

Think accessibility first

Products should work well for everyone, not just for the easiest use case.

06

Measure outcomes

Focus on the impact on users and the business, not just on shipping features.
